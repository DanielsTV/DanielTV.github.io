DOTA 2 TEAM VICTUS-PRO HAVING FUN WITH THEIR OPONENT

MANILA -- He may have found himself in hot water for his rude and uncalled for comments on Daniel Padilla. But young actor Paul Salas can at least console himself with the thought of having the support of his rumored girlfriend, ex-"Pinoy Big Brother" housemate Barbie Imperial. Moments after fans caught Salas's swipe at Padilla, in which he clowned the "La Luna Sangre" star for being agitated by a hard foul during a basketball game, Imperial took to Twitter to come to his defense. "Good times or bad times, I got his back," Imperial wrote. "Even if you bash me, I just wanna show you guys that bad comments won't stop me. I know him." She was apparently responding to some harsh posts about her relationship with Salas. Her bashers poked fun at her post last week, in which she could be seen cuddling with Salas.
